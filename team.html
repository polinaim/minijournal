About Us



We are 15 Doctoral Candidates participating in the European Glaucoma Research Training program for Accelerating the Approach to Advanced Glaucoma (EGRET-AAA). As part of the EGRET-AAA program, we are fortunate to learn from leading European experts in molecular biology, ophthalmology, and visual neuroscience. Our training involves a variety of high-level courses and hands-on experiences across different academic and industrial settings.


While we are focused on advancing research in potential therapies and better assesment of glaucoma, we also recognize the importance of communicating science effectively. This journal is our modest attempt to share what we learn along the way, making the fascinating world of visual science a little more approachable for everyone.


Our Purpose

Our goal is to help bridge the gap between scientific research and the general public. We believe that understanding visual science shouldn't be limited to experts. Through this journal, we aim to:


  • • Explain complex concepts in simple terms
  • • Highlight interesting research developments in glaucoma and visual science
  • • Encourage curiosity about the science behind our vision


We invite you to join us on this journey as we explore the complexities of visual science and work towards a future with better treatments for glaucoma. Thank you for being part of our community!
